COME TOGETHER ACT TOGETHER

Join Our Fight To End Period Shame

Menstruation is a natural bodily function and yet period shame along with lack of access to menstrual hygiene products and awareness continues to hold back millions. A staggering 88% of menstruating women in India use unhygienic materials such as ash, dried leaves, old rags and newspapers to absorb menstrual discharge. We have partnered with CRY - Child Rights and You, a leading Indian non-profit to bring menstrual products, education and awareness to adolescent girls and women in slum communities hit by the pandemic. Through #DropThePWord, we are starting a national conversation around period shame and its adverse effects on the lives of people. Every donation brings us closer to reach our goal of providing menstrual hygiene products to 10,000+ people across 4,500 households and every pledge brings us closer to winning our fight against period shame. You can also donate sealed packets of period products in our Red Period Bins across our all our stores which will be donated to local shelters through CRY.

Join us online or in-store to support us in this fight..
